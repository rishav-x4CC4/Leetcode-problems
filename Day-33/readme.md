## 💡 Key Learnings

### Reverse Linked List
- Learned how to reverse a singly linked list iteratively.
- Used three pointers (`prev`, `curr`, `next`) to reverse links safely.
- Constant extra space solution.

### Middle of the Linked List
- Used the Fast & Slow Pointer technique.
- When the fast pointer reaches the end, the slow pointer points to the middle.
- Elegant one-pass solution.
